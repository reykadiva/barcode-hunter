## 1. Overview

This document captures ideas for future features and expansions. Ideas here are not committed — they require evaluation against the Core Design Principles before implementation.

### Evaluation Criteria

Every idea should be evaluated against:

1. **Does it serve the pet?** — Core Design Principle #1
2. **Does it strengthen the player-pet bond?**
3. **Is it cozy, not stressful?** — Core Design Principle #5
4. **Does it celebrate the physical world?** — Core Design Principle #6

## 2. Near-Term Ideas (Post-Launch)

### 2.1 Mini-Games

#### Catch the Fish

A simple tap game where Scan Chan catches falling fish. Scanned products become power-ups that slow time or multiply fish.

**Evaluation**:
- Serves pet: Yes (play activity)
- Strengthens bond: Moderate (shared fun)
- Cozy: Yes (no fail state)
- Physical world: Moderate (products as power-ups)

**Status**: Under consideration

#### Memory Match

A card-matching game using product images from the player's collection. Matching pairs earn treats for Scan Chan.

**Evaluation**:
- Serves pet: Yes (mental stimulation)
- Strengthens bond: Moderate
- Cozy: Yes
- Physical world: Strong (uses player's actual products)

**Status**: Under consideration

#### Cat Nap

An idle game that runs while Scan Chan sleeps. Player can check in to see dream animations and earn passive XP.

**Evaluation**:
- Serves pet: Yes (rest is important)
- Strengthens bond: Low (passive)
- Cozy: Very
- Physical world: Low

**Status**: Under consideration

### 2.2 Social Features

#### Pet Sharing

Send your pet to visit a friend's room for a few hours. Both pets play together and earn bonus affection.

**Status**: Requires social infrastructure — deferred

#### Gift System

Send products to friends' pets. The recipient's pet enjoys a treat from you.

**Status**: Requires social infrastructure — deferred

#### Leaderboards

Weekly leaderboards for most unique products scanned, longest streaks, highest level.

**Status**: May conflict with "no pressure" principle — needs careful design

### 2.3 Seasonal Events

#### Holiday Decorations

Time-limited decorations for the pet's room during holidays (Christmas, Halloween, Valentine's, etc.)

**Status**: Planned

#### Limited-Time Missions

Special missions during holidays with unique rewards.

**Status**: Planned

#### Seasonal Pet Outfits

Cute outfits the pet can wear during special occasions.

**Status**: Planned

## 3. Mid-Term Ideas (6-12 Months)

### 3.1 Multi-Pet System

#### Second Pet Adoption

Adopt a second pet (possibly a different species). The two pets interact and keep each other company.

**Design Considerations**:
- How do two pets share a room?
- Do they have separate stats?
- Can they play together?

**Status**: Major feature — requires extensive design

#### Pet Mentor System

A veteran pet can "mentor" a new pet, providing bonuses to the new pet's progression.

**Status**: Interesting concept — needs design work

### 3.2 Advanced Customization

#### Pet Outfits & Accessories

Hats, scarves, collars, and other accessories for the pet.

**Status**: Planned

#### Room Themes

Complete room themes (cozy cabin, modern apartment, garden, etc.)

**Status**: Planned

#### Custom Pet Colors

Allow players to customize their pet's fur colors and patterns.

**Status**: May conflict with "unique personality" — needs design consideration

### 3.3 Pet Jobs

#### Adventures

Send Scan Chan on idle adventures. They return with souvenirs and stories.

**Status**: Under consideration

#### Jobs Based on Personality

Different personality types unlock different jobs:
- Foodie → Food critic
- Adventurous → Explorer
- Social → Greeter

**Status**: Interesting concept — needs design work

## 4. Long-Term Ideas (1-2 Years)

### 4.1 AR Integration

#### AR Pet View

See Scan Chan in your real room through your phone camera.

**Status**: Technically complex — deferred

#### AR Product Interaction

Scan products in AR and watch Scan Chan interact with the virtual product in your space.

**Status**: Technically complex — deferred

### 4.2 Cross-Platform

#### Pet Export

Export your pet as an avatar that can appear in other games or apps.

**Status**: Requires partnerships — deferred

#### Pet NFT (Optional)

Optional blockchain-based pet ownership for players who want true ownership and portability.

**Status**: Controversial — requires careful consideration. Never pay-to-win.

### 4.3 Community Features

#### Pet Playdates

Your pet visits another player's pet for a playdate.

**Status**: Requires social infrastructure — deferred

#### Community Challenges

Collective goals where all players contribute (e.g., "Scan 1 million unique products as a community").

**Status**: Interesting concept — needs design

#### Pet Beauty Contests

Players vote on cutest pets in various categories.

**Status**: May create unhealthy competition — needs careful design

### 4.4 Narrative Expansion

#### Pet Dreams

Story sequences that play when the pet sleeps, revealing memories and personality.

**Status**: Under consideration

#### Memory Cinema

Watch a cinematic replay of your journey with Scan Chan.

**Status**: Under consideration

#### AI-Generated Pet Diary

Scan Chan writes diary entries based on your scan history and interactions.

**Status**: Technically interesting — requires AI integration

## 5. Wildcard Ideas

### 5.1 Pet Swap Event

Once a year, players can temporarily swap pets with a friend to experience how others care for their companions.

**Status**: Wildcard — may be too complex

### 5.2 Time Capsule

Bury a memory capsule with your favorite memories. Unlock it in exactly one year to see how much has changed.

**Status**: Wildcard — emotionally compelling

### 5.3 Legacy Mode

When a pet reaches max level, they can "mentor" a new kitten. The original pet remains, but you start a new journey.

**Status**: Wildcard — interesting endgame concept

### 5.4 Real-World Integration

#### Grocery List Sync

Scan Chan suggests products based on your pet's favorite categories.

**Status**: Wildcard — useful but complex

#### Recipe Integration

Scan ingredients while cooking. Scan Chan "helps" you cook.

**Status**: Wildcard — fun concept

## 6. Rejected Ideas

Ideas that were considered but rejected for not aligning with Core Design Principles.

### 6.1 Competitive PvP

**Idea**: Pets compete against each other in challenges.

**Rejected Because**: Violates "cozy, never stressful" principle. Creates anxiety and pressure.

### 6.2 Pet Death/Abandonment

**Idea**: Pets can pass away or leave if neglected.

**Rejected Because**: Violates "cozy, never stressful" principle. Creates guilt and fear.

### 6.3 Pay-to-Win Mechanics

**Idea**: Buy premium food for faster progression.

**Rejected Because**: Violates the ethical hook model. Creates inequality.

### 6.4 Gacha/Loot Boxes

**Idea**: Random chance to get rare items.

**Rejected Because**: Encourages unhealthy spending and gambling behavior.

### 6.5 Daily Login Punishment

**Idea**: Pet loses stats or becomes sad if you don't log in daily.

**Rejected Because**: Violates "Welcome Back" philosophy. Creates obligation, not desire.
